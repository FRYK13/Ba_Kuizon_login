What made you go for this advocacy?

Gender inequality affects everyone. Gender equality prevents violence against women and girls. Regardless of where you live in, gender equality is a fundamental human right. Advancing gender equality is critical to all areas of a healthy society, from reducing poverty to promoting the health, education, protection and the well-being of girls and boys. Everyone benefits from gender equality.

How you can help:

  • Share household chores and childcare equally
  • Watch for signs of Domestice violence
  • Reject chauvinist and racist attitudes
  • Listen and Reflect
  • Do not tolerate sexual harrassment
  • Discuss gender equality with family members and children.
  • Promote gender equality at home.
  • Advocate for equity.
  • Make sure learning materials represent everyone.
  • Use appropriate language.
